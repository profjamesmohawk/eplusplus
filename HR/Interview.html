COMP-10132 Interview Tutorial

You will create a structured interview and submit your work to Canvas for grading.

Process

  1. Select the job for which you will be interviewing. It may be any job, but it should be one with which you are familiar. Here are some jobs you might choose:
    1. Help-Desk Technician
    2. Shipper/Receiver
    3. Retail Computer Sales Person
  2. Compose three interview questions
  3. For each interview question provide three sample answers: excellent, good, and poor
    • These sample answers serve as a grading scheme for your interview
  4. Submit your work to the eLearn dropbox

Notes:

  • All questions must be behavioural.
  • Recall that historical questions are better than hypothetical.
  • Submit your work as a PDF.
