Financial Modelling

We will build a trivial financial model. Our goal is to explore tools and techniques. An accurate model is beyond the scope of this course.

Scenario

You have authored a hot new app. It's called WordleGo and it combines the best of Wordle and Pokemon Go. We will build a trivial financial model to see the effects of growth and pricing.

Assumptions:

  • You currently have 10K users.
  • The model will cover 24 months at the month level.
  • Your user base will grow exponentially for 6 months then decline exponentially for 18 months.
  • You may choose to charge a onetime license fee, a monthly fee, or a combination of the two.
  • Any revenue from advertising or data harvesting will be recorded as a monthly fee.
  • When calculating revenue or cost based on number of users, we will always use the number of users at the start of the month.

Assignment:

  1. Complete the template worksheet.
  2. Use your completed spreadsheet to answer the quiz questions on Canvas.
