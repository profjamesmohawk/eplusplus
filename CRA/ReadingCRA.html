Reading Assignment: Canadian Revenue Agency

The Canadian Revenue Agency (CRA) publishes reports of tax convictions on their web site. For this assignment you will read three of these cases and answer some questions.

  1. Read the cases:
    • The Dentist
    • The Corrupt Employee
    • The Lazy Person
  2. Complete the Canvas quiz "CRA Tax Convictions Reading Assignment"

Here are the question you will have to answer:

  1. Who's fraud is consistent with buying a laptop for home use but charging it to the company?
  2. Who's fraud could also be described as kick-backs or bribes?
  3. Who did Revenue Canada warn before charging?
  4. In your opinion, were the penalties assessed fair?
