Reading Assignment: The Economist

"The Economist" is as London based newspaper that focuses on world affairs. It is well regarded as a voice of thoughtful comment. For this assignment you will read one article and answer some questions.

  1. Read the article "The business of survival". The online version can be found here an archived PDF version can be found here
  2. Complete the Canvas quiz "Economist Reading Assignment"

Here are the question you will have to answer:

  1. Who does "The Economist" think will benefit from the pandemic?
  2. How many days worth of inventory does Apple have?
  3. Does "The Economist" think that the pandemic will motivate companies to move manufacturing closer to their home bases?
  4. What fraction of America's industries have become more concentrated since the 1990's?
