COMP-10132 Strike The Test

The last 15 minutes of this period will be devoted to a quiz on Industrial Labour Relations focusing on the rules governing the certification of unions.

Special Grading Considerations

  • If you can successfully strike the test, everyone will score 60%
  • If you attempt to strike the test, but fail
    • Everyone who writes will get their true score
    • Everyone who participates in the failed strike will get a chance to write a slightly harder quiz later
  • If there is no labour disruption, everyone will get a bonus mark.

Process

  1. Take a few minutes to determine what you think is best for the class and best for you
  2. Your instructor will allot presentation time to anyone who wishes to address the class (time allowing)
  3. All students will leave the class to enjoy a short break
  4. Students who wish to write the test will return at the time indicated by your instructor.
