Which Car?

You need to buy a company car to visit the customers of you small software company. Your biggest customer is a Toyota dealer so you've decided on either a Camry or a Camry Hybrid.

  1. The Camry can be had on a 4 year lease for $96/week.
  2. The Camry uses 8.1 l/km in the city and 5.7 l/km on the highway.
  3. The Hybrid can be had on a 4 year lease for $115/month.
  4. The Hybrid uses 4.9 l/km in the city and 4.8 l/km on the highway.
  1. Which car should you purchase and why?

There are three variables that impact your decision: gas price, weekly km in the city, weekly km on the highway. Problems like this are great for spreadsheets. A Spreadsheet makes it easy to run many scenarios.
