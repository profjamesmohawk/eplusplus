COMP-10132 Negotiation Tutorial

For this exercise you will play one of three roles in a negotiation.

Process

  1. Form groups of three as directed by your instructor.
  2. Receive your confidential negotiation instructions from your instructor
  3. Negotiate as directed by your instructor
  4. As a group prepare a quick-pitch describing:
    1. the strategies you used
    2. the results you got
    3. what would you do differently
