COMP-10132 Market Research Tutorial

Many say that formal market research is the only way to properly know your customers. Let's conduct our own little experiment.

Experiment Description

Today we will compare the effectiveness of interviews verses questionnaires. Each group will work to answer the question "What do students think about the new Mohawk athletics and recreation centre?"

    As a group:

  1. Create 4 questions that can answered yes/no.
  2. Create 2 questions that can be answered with full sentences.

    3. Individually (on campus):

  3. Spend 15 minutes administering your yes/no questions.
  4. Spend 15 minutes administering your short answer questions.

    5. As a group:

  5. Compare your answers.
  6. Prepare to present your findings:
    1. Which method allowed you to survey the greatest number of participants?
    2. Which method would be best for producing pretty charts?
    3. Which method would be best administered by a co-op student?
    4. Which method would be best administered by Rob McIsaac?
    5. Did you find out anything surprising?
    6. How would you like to be surveyed?
    7. If Rob McIsaac called you at home, would you take time to talk to him?
    8. If a call centre agent called you at home, would you "answer some questions about Mohawk College"?
