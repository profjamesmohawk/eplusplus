Tutotial One - Questions


  1. Five workers can dig ten holes in two hours.
    How long will it take one worker to dig one hole?
    How quickly could two workers dig one hole?
    How quickly could 50 workers dig one hole?

  2. Sally wants to get fit. She can join a gym on one of two plans.
    1. $10 initiation and $5/visit
    2. $500/year for unlimited visits.
    How many visits must Sally make for the $500 membership to be the less expensive option?

  3. Consider the following table of compile times:
    4. Application Time (seconds)
    HelloWorld.c 10
    foo.c (1,000 lines) 12
    Bar.c(2,000 lines) 14
    How long will it take to compile a 5,000 line program?

  4. What are the odds of flipping a coin and getting 3 heads in a row?
