COMP-10132 Business in Canada Tutorial Questions

Your team will answer the questions given to you by your instructor and present your answer to the class.

Process

  1. Form groups as directed by your instructor.
  2. while (true) {
    1. Answer your question
    2. Present your answer
    }

Questions:

  1. For each of the businesses listed below make a case that it should be nationalized or privatized as directed by your instructor:
    1. Liquor stores
    2. Colleges
    3. Lotteries
    4. Nuclear Energy
    5. Auto Insurance
    6. Dog sitting

  2. Describe 2 ways the Canadian government can stimulate the economy.

  3. Explain how raising the tax on cigarettes could actually reduce government revenues rather than increase them? (Hint: consider a VERY large increase)

  4. Give an example of:
    1. Collusion
    2. Pyramid Scheme
    3. Ponzi Scheme
    4. Bait-and-switch
    5. Predatory Pricing
    6. Sales Price Maintenance

  5. Describe an event that would raise the value of the Canadian dollar.

  6. Describe an event that would lower the value of the Canadian dollar.

  7. Who benefits from a low dollar?

  8. Who benefits from a high dollar?
